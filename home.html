Big Manni

Hi, I am Manish. Friends call me Manni and my comic friends gave me a new name BIG Manni. Big Manni is actually very small. I am a chick magnet. Chicks take me home and stick me on their fridge.

Also, support my friends and comedy mentors Maria Herman and Tony Calabrese. Thanks for visiting my page. Come sometime see me perform.
