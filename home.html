Big Manni

Hi, I am Manish. Family and friends call me Manni. My comic friends gave me a new name BIG Manni. Big Manni is actually very small. I am a chick magnet. Chicks take me home and stick me on their fridge.

Also, support my friends and comedy mentors Maria Herman and Tony Calabrese. Thanks for visiting my page. Come sometime to see me perform. Thanks! Manish.
